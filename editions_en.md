## Where to Find 

**Primo RPA Studio Community** can be requested on the [official website](https://primo-rpa.ru/) of Primo RPA. Although this edition does not require a license, projects can only be executed within the Studio - the Primo RPA Robot application necessitates a separate license.

**Primo RPA Studio Enterprise** is a commercial version, available upon request. During the installation of the Studio, a license will be requested - this can only be obtained with a valid contract. To acquire a license, formulate a request and send it to the vendor's email. Further details on how to do this, as well as information on the types of licenses, can be found in the article [Launching Studio Enterprise](https://docs.primo-rpa.ru/primo-rpa/primo-studio/enterprise).
